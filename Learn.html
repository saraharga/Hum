What is Music Therapy

The therapeutic use of music (as to reduce anxiety, improve cognitive functioning, promote physical rehabilitation, or enhance interpersonal communication) that typically involves listening to music, singing, playing musical instruments, or composing music.

  • Discussing music

Benefits of Music Therapy

Music therapy may be beneficial for people experiencing:

  • Trauma-related disorders
  • Mood disorders
  • Anxiety disorders
  • Dementia
  • Substance abuse disorders
  • Schizophrenia / psychotic disorders
  • Movement disorders
  • Cardiac / respiration problems

Music therapy is also often used to help children and adolescents:

  • Develop their identities
  • Improve their communication skills
  • Learn to regulate their emotions
  • Recover from trauma
  • Self-reflect
