About Hum

Hum is a web app that helps individuals guide a musical therapy session to help a family member or friend battling with Alzheimer's disease. Our program designs specific sessions based on the user's preferences and works as a tool so everyone can introduce this type of therapy to anyone struggling, letting them enjoy all the benefits musical therapy has to offer.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help bridge the gap in medical services by making music therapy accessible to everyone, give personalised recommendations and provide the benefits of music therapy and relevant background information.

The Importance

Research suggests that listening to or singing songs can provide emotional and behavioral benefits for people with Alzheimer's disease, since musical memories are often preserved because key brain areas linked to musical memory are relatively undamaged by the disease.
